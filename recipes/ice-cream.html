Ice Cream Recipe

Description

This is a "recipe" for store bought ice cream. It's delicious and impossible to mess up.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite ice cream
  • Spoon and ice cream scoop (optional)

Steps

  1. Take the ice cream out of the freezer
  2. Use an ice cream scoop and put us much ice cream as you'd like into a bowl (optional)
  3. Take the ice cream to a comfy place and enjoy!
