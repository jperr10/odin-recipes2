Hamburger Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for grilling a hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Frozen hamburger patty
  • Two halves of a hamburger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Warm your grill to 450.
  2. Place frozen hamburger patty on the grill.
  3. After 4 minutes, use a spatula to flip the hamburger onto its other side.
  4. After another 4 minutes remove the hamburger from the grill and place on the bottom half of the hamburger bun.
  5. Top burger with and desired condiments and place top half of bun on top. Enjoy!
